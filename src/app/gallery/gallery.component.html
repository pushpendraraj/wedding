By Train :

Venue : Reached at JHANSI railway station by train it is approx 30KM away from CHIRGAON, from here you can prefer TRAIN for CHIRGAON or you can take bus from JHANSI bus stand it will take hardly 45MIN.

By Bus :

Venue : Reached at JHANSI bus stand take a bus going towards KANPUR. it will take hardly 45MIN (30KM approx) drop you at CHIRGAON.
